Life at MANIT

The Sacred Journey

by Krishna Chandan

    As your cab takes you up on towards the main campus of MANIT for the very first time, you would have a sense of excitement and completeness coupled with enthusiasm and eagerness for this new journey of your life to begin. Your new made friends behave with you at the best of their behavioural metre and you would also reciprocate it towards each and every new person that you meet. Soon you get your admission process raced off with the unconditional support that you receive from the seniors you meet. Within no time you get allotted into your rooms waiting for the clock of your college life to start! 

    Life in MANIT like life in any college would be a rollercoaster of a ride irrespective of the talents, desires and interests that you possess. A four year old journey at the very peak of your teenage hood would be bound to have its up’s and down’s, for it is the very beauty of life!

    Your life in MANIT is largely determined by your actions that you choose to make at various stages of your college life. Your sparked feeling for that cute girl or boy in your class may or may not last forever, the friend that you had plans to hang out with throughout at the beginning of your college life may or may not stay with you but the very path of the actions that you choose to make ends up being inerasable. 

    MANIT always provides you with places like OCTANE, AMUL and NESCAFE with celebrative moods to enjoy your happy times as well places like the MANIT LAKE , NCC grounds and GS to quench your need for 'Alone' time during your slow and low days. That small inspirational talk, that little start-up plan, that intense debate made at these places with the best of your friends stay with you throughout your life to look back and smile upon. When you feel hungry, this 650 acre campus boasts of having various night canteens to enjoy a snack with your mates, a great sports arena to feed your cricket and football fever,

    A huge auditorium to cheer and boo the performers during those special events. These are the actions that that you would cherish years and years down the lane. 

    Disappointments and Despair are part and parcel of life, but when you feel so this college and its students have it in them to pull you out of it in no time making you look back and laugh upon them. Always remember, there is an answer to every problem in MANIT! Be it low marks, college fights, family problems etc. That’s what makes this college that extra bit special! At the end of the course, one could always vouch for great times and great memories made at every corner of this college to enthusiastic freshers like you. So enjoy this experience to the fullest of its potential and discover your transformation into young and bright individuals in this beautiful institution.

The most cherishable years of my life

by Megha Saxena

    Maulana Azad National Institute Of Technology Bhopal (MACT / MANIT / NIT-Bhopal) was established as an institute of national importance under the NIT-act 2007.It started in 1960 and functionally inaugurated in 1962 by Pt Jawahar Lal Nehru.

    The institute is situated in the heart of Bhopal (Mata Mandir) on a beautiful plateau with a verdant 650-acre campus.It has 10 hostels,excellent food and lodging facilities with 24 hour security.MANIT's Computer Center, Canteen, Workshops, Athletic Ground, Sports Complex, Gym, Energy Center and the open air auditorium are the places where students enjoy a competitive yet healthy environment. Life in MANIT is a perfect combination of academics and extra curricular activities. MANIT justifies its logo 'vidhya param bhushanam' i.e; knowledge is the greatest jewel by providing good acedemics and excellent placements. The most exciting moments of life in MANIT are because of the wide variety of student organisations (societies) and the annual fests. It has more than 25 societies like Rashtraay, Pixel ,Drishtant etc. and fests like tooryanaad, maffick, sportsmania which offer ample amount of opportunituies to all students.

    Apart from all this Manit  gives its students the 'attitude' to handle any problem and the 'confidence' to be different. It develops a student’s 'personality'  which they will carry throughout their life, leaving them with good friends and sweet memories for life.The best thing about MANIT is "It lets the kid inside you breathe, fly in its full swing, and still, gives one the maturity and affection of a grown-up."  Life in MANIT may sometimes be tough , sometimes easy, at times boring and at other times quite exiting but overall life here is beautiful or rather 'Too good to be true!’ These four years here will probably be the best 4-years of every Manitian!

MANIT Life

by Purvanshi Yadav

Regarding MANIT the first question which comes to one's mind is ragging. In MANIT, we have a concept of zero ragging and at the time of admission you are required to fill an anti ragging form. A number in case of any emergency will also be provided so that the moment you encounter any kind of ragging, the number given can be dialled and help will be provided. But no such problem has been registered in the past few years.

The campus has a dispensary with a qualified doctor and an ambulance available 24*7. Also, there are many hospitals in close vicinity of the campus.

For fooding purposes, other than the hostel mess, there is a canteen, a cafe, Nescafe outlets and an Amul parlour in the campus itself. Other than this there are many good restaurants and food joints nearby at a distance of a kilometer or two.

MANIT is located in the city itself and good coaching institutes are available nearby. You have buses and autos available just outside the campus to enable movement in and around the city.
