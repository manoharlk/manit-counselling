ABOUT US

    This Website is an initiative of the Students of MANIT(NIT)Bhopal, who were just like you when you passed JEE Mains and are trepidatious about what to do during the admission into the college. So, we decided to help students and parents/guardians during the admission and this Website is the first step to it.
   We are a set of dedicated and hardworking students who vow to help and guide you throughout the Admission process in the college. We run under the name "MAARG" meaning “the way”, acting as your guides who show you the right way. Inspired by Abraham Lincoln, our motto is "Of The students, By the Students and For the Students". If you have any doubts regarding MANIT or Admission process, you can mail us at maarg.nitb@gmail.com or contact us on our facebook page.
