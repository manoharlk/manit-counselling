ABOUT US

    This Website is an Initiative from the Students of MANIT(NIT)Bhopal,who just as you have passed JEE Mains and boggled what to do during the admission into the college. So We decided to help those students and parents/guardians during the admission and this Website is the first step to it.
    We are dedicated and hardworking Students who vowed to guide you during the Admission process in the college too. We run under the Name " MAARG" and as the name depicts who show you the right way.
Our motto is " Off The students, By the Students and For the Students". If you have any doubts regarding MANIT or Admission process, mail us at maarg.nitb@gmail.com or contact us at our facebook page.
