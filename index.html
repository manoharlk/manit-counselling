How does the system of allotment of rooms to freshmen work?

MANIT follows pretty good system for allotting rooms to freshmen. Hostel Authority randomly allot the rooms and the roommates are decided in order of their registration in the hostel. Generally 4 people have to share one room alongwith cupboards. In rare cases, it may be 3 people sharing one room. The reason to adopt this system is that the students should interact more with their roommates and other new students. It is helpful for one gets to know his/her batchmate. Learn other's culture and language ,if they belong to some other state or country. Moreover, it helps cultivate new bonds of friendship.

Where do we get textbooks from?

It makes sense to wait a couple of days for the professor to specify which books they will be following. Although, hardly any changes are there and you can ask your seniors which books to recommend. Here are the ways to get text books: 1) They are issued at the institute library after your card is issued. You can get the form online and submit it with your photograph and admission card to get your library card. 2) There are book bank at MANIT. You can issue twice from book banks per year. Books are issued for a semester's duration. Issuing books from book bank requires form fillings each year. Priority on the basis of category is given. (SC/ST >OBC > GEN) 3) You may also borrow books from seniors or purchase from them at half rate. Seniors are usually keen to give out books. 4) You may purchase books from the retailers from local market of Bhopal. They follow a return policy and refund some amount on returning books after a semester is over.

Tell me more about department change.

MANIT encourages meritorious students by offering the option of a change of department at the end of first year. It depends upon the number of seats available in the quota through which you took admission. This requires a good CGPA at the end of 2nd semester. However, you must understand that only a handful of students can get a department change, so don't bank upon getting one and choose a department in which you could plan your career.

How much would we need to study at Manit?

The amount of study one has to put in varies from individual to individual. Proffesors cover all the important topics in their lectures, studying from their notes can fetch you decent marks, but that requires regular attendance. If you are aiming for a department change, you probably will have to study a bit more than you usually would. Life at NIT is not just about academic excellence but about overall development. There are many workshops held every weekend and various events organised by different societies, in which you must participate.
